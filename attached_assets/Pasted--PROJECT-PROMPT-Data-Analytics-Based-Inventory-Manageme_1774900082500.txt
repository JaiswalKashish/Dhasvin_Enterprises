**PROJECT PROMPT: Data Analytics Based Inventory Management System with Smart Invoice Upload and Premium SaaS UI**

Build a complete full-stack web application named:

**“Data Analytics Based Inventory Management System with Smart Invoice Upload and Business Insights”**

This should be a **real-time, industry-level SaaS web application** for a retail shop, hardware store, warehouse, or stock-based business.

---

## 🎯 PROJECT OBJECTIVE

Create a smart inventory system that helps businesses:

- Manage products and stock efficiently
- Upload invoices (CSV/Excel/PDF-ready)
- Automatically update inventory
- Track purchases and sales through excel upload features so that it can directly fetch the information
- Analyze business performance through graphs
- Identify low stock and dead stock
- Generate reports and insights
- Generate bills/invoices with print functionality

---

### Frontend

- Tailwind CSS

- ShadCN UI or reusable components

- Node.js

### Data Visualization

- Recharts

### Reports & Export

- date-fns

### Analytics

- JavaScript-based analytics (simple business insights)
